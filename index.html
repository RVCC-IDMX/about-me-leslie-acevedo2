Image hover effects

Image hover effects are a visual change / transiston that happens when you move your cursor over an image. These hover effects are a fun tool that makes the user viewing your webpage more interactive. Alot of websties use this technique to provide more information about a specific thing or image.

With Image hover effects there are so many different combinations, I took some things that I read about, saw in videos on youtube while reasearching this topic and I used it to create a hover effect on my photo gallery , I loved the way it turned out because now my webpage looks nice.
